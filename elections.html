Due to coronavirus restrictions, the HVCA's elections will be done virtually and via paper ballots. Voting begins on Friday, March 19, 2021 from 10:00am and close at 10:00am Sunday, March 21, 2021. The two days period is to allow everyone enough time to vote, bearing in mind that the internet service can be very unreliable at times.

Note that members should have a password if they are to use the electronic ballot. Each HVCA member of good standing was assigned a specific password for voting and will be contacted by a member of the Nominating Committee between March 16-18, 2021. Voting must occur ONLY ONCE per member in good standing. If a person opt to vote via electronic ballot they CANNOT vote via paper ballot and vice versa. Due to the pandemic, we are providing to a limited numbers of members an opportunity to vote via paper ballot. For those requesting a paper ballot to vote, please contact Mrs. Ralph at 876-439-2654 to make arrangements.

The Nominating Committee Will collate all ballots (paper and electronic) and the Clerk will present this information at the March 21, 2021 Annual General Meeting.

Cast your vote and make your voice heard!
